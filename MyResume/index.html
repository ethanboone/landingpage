This application was built as a group project for Codeworks, and our group completed the app in about two weeks. We used regex search functionality, geospatial queries with GeoJSON, and many to many data relationships in our application. The core functionality of the app involved users listing items as available to be borrowered, and other user's could request to borrow an item. Our group also included a two way rating system to rate the borrower as well as the lender. The application was made with a Vue.js front end, Node.js back end and Mongoose/MongoDb for the ORM and database respectively.
